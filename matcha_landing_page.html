Ceremonial Uji-Source

"The energy of Saigon and Hanoi isn't just about speed—it's about a new kind of mindfulness. I saw Vietnamese youth craving something that spoke to their aesthetic while honoring quality."

Matcha Neo started as a rebellion against standard, sugary coffee culture. We sourced the finest ceremonial grade matcha from Uji and brought it to the neon-lit streets. It's not just a drink; it's a social pulse, a vibrant green light in the dark.
